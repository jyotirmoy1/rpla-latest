  • Ravi Prakash

Following several higher management roles in leading organiztions over the last three decades - Ravi has finally built RPLA with a vision to offer high quality services with compassion and empathy.


Past & Present

  • 1985

    Partner, J.C. Bhalla & Co.

    Completed his articleship training (as part of chartered accountancy course) with J. C. Bhalla & Co., Chartered Accountants, New Delhi, and upon qualifying as a chartered accountant in February 1985, Ravi kick-started his career with them. Ravi was promoted as a Partner in October 1985.

  • 1990

    Head of Taxation, Max India Ltd.

    Group head of taxation of Max India Ltd, New Delhi (now known as Max Financial Services Limited subsequent to demerger of Max India Ltd into three listed companies). Wide ranging role comprising of various aspects of corporate, tax, accounting, disclosures and regulatory laws, for various Max Group entities including Promoter entities.

  • 2003

    Senior Manager, PricewaterhouseCoopers.

    Head of Knowledge Management with the key responsibility of training and guiding the chartered accountants engaged in direct tax practice. Member of the core team of PwC India regarding various tax positions. Liasoning with various industry bodies on issues concerning tax and policy changes.

  • 2007

    Senior Partner, AZB & Partners

    Member of the Tax and Forensic Practices of AZB. Vast experience in tax, legal, accounting, corporate and regulatory matters. Regularly counseled several international and domestic corporations, Fortune 500 companies, accounting firms, PE investors, FIIs/FPIs etc. on tax issues arising from cross-border transactions as well as matters concerning foreign direct investment, mergers, acquisitions and divestments. Also advised on international tax, tax treaties, succession planning, corporate & business restructuring, joint ventures and corporate & regulatory laws, accounting and disclosure obligations.

  • 2019

    Managing Partner, RPLA.

    Founder and Managing Partner of RPLA. Specializes in tax structuring, planning, management, succession planning and related corporate, regulatory and accounting aspects.
